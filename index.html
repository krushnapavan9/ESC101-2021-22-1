Academic Integrity

  • You may discuss concepts with classmates, but all submissions must be your own or your team's work when teamwork is permitted.
  • You may not search online for existing solutions related to the assignments, even as a reference.
  • Students caught cheating or plagiarizing will be severely penalized. The penalty includes the possibility of failing the course and being reported to the Institute.

Please refer to the Anti-Cheating Policies page. The use of unfair means by any student will be severely punished. Examples of possible penalties are grade deductions, awarding F grade award, and referral to institute disciplinary committee.

Evaluation Scheme

  • Weekly labs (best 10/11) - 70%
  • Three quizzes - 30%
There will be no mid-semester or end-semester exams.
